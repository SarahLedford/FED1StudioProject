Where the Magic Begins

At Threads of Magic, it is our passion and goal to teach people of all ages, young and old, the art of crochet and fiber arts. A team of twelve, we have you covered every step of the way from ordering supplies to start your adventure or to continue along on it, to learning how to create your wildest imaginings and even tips and tricks on how to start your own business selling what you have put so much love and time into.
